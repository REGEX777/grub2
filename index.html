The Mission

April 1945. As the Allies make their final push into Nazi Germany, battle-hardened U.S. Army Sergeant Don "Wardaddy" Collier commands a Sherman tank named FURY and its five-man crew on a deadly mission behind enemy lines.

Outgunned and outnumbered, Wardaddy (Brad Pitt) and his men—Bible (Shia LaBeouf), Gordo (Michael Peña), Coon-Ass (Jon Bernthal), and rookie typist Norman (Logan Lerman)—must confront the brutal realities of war when they face a battalion of SS troops in a desperate last stand.
